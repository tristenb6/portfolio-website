The Golden City

San Francisco, CA Spring 2025

Sunset in San Francisco cranks up the saturation on every bay reflection. These frames chase that glow across ferry decks, cable car lines, and Market Street glass.

Minolta Freedom 70EX Kodak Gold 200
Scroll to explore

On the Move

Shot from the Embarcadero up through Nob Hill. I followed the trolley lines, hopping on and off to keep pace with the evening light and the way it wrapped around the Bay Bridge.

Soundtrack

Spoiler alert: Anderson .Paak on loop, mixed with seagulls and market chatter. The groove kept me walking and helped sync each frame to the pulse of the city.

Behind the Frame

Field Notes

Mist on the bay meant doubling down on skylines and silhouettes. I leaned into backlight whenever the fog broke, bracketing a few frames to hold highlights.

Experiment

Shot the same scene at ISO 200 and 400 to stretch the negatives, then scanned with a warm curve to keep the amber glow true to memory.
