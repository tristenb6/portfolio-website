Kick things off with a lively intro. Use chips above to set a vibe (movie night, food crawl, etc.). Below are interactive bits you can remix per post.

Tip: Use .image-link anchors to open images in a lightbox via Chocolat (already wired globally).

Quick Takes

  • Best scene/plate: describe your favorite moment or flavor.
  • Vibe check: cozy, chaotic, cinematic, nostalgic—your call.
  • Would you recommend? Who would love this?

Spoiler Box

Keep surprises optional. Toggle reveals with Bootstrap collapse.

Reveal Spoilers
Plot twist or secret sauce goes here. Keep it fun and respectful for readers who opt in.

Info Tabs

  • Director / Studio / Runtime
  • Genres / Rating
  • Where to watch (if relevant)
  • Restaurant / Cuisine / Price range
  • Signature dishes
  • Location & vibe
  • Behind-the-scenes trivia
  • Easter eggs or local lore
  • Pairings (snacks, playlists)

Quotes & Callouts

“Add a pull quote for flavor or insight. Keep it punchy.”
Use callouts to summarize verdicts, tips, or how-to steps.
